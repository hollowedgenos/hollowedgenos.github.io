## Specifics
One of the player characters, ASTR-1D, came with four abilities:
- Hammer Charge
- Tornado Spin
- Hammer Ride
- Company Mandated Dance Session

<b>Hammer Charge</b> is a primary attack, the player character can choose to either hold the mouse button down for big damage or immediately press it to get a quick hit in.

<br><b>Tornado Spin</b> is a secondary attack in which the player does immediate damage to all enemies around them while drawing them in their attack radius.

<br><b>Hammer Ride</b> is a movement-based ability where the player traverses a short distance in a burst of speed, knocking over and dealing damage to any enemies in their way.

<br><b>Company Mandated Dance Session</b> is a support-focused ultimate ability that stuns all enemies around the player for a short amount of time.
