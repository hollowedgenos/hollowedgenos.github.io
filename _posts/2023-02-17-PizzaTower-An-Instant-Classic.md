# It's Pizza Time!
{: .text-center}

Pizza Tower is a fast-paced 2D platformer that-after many years of development-released this year on January 26th, 2023. At the time of posting this blog, I currently have 10 hours in the game, and I am not even close to completing it. I usually reserve my judgement of a game after I have played it, but this is one of the exceptions. Pizza Tower is a heart-pounding, adrenaline-infused experience that I recommend to anyone who loves the platforming genre. It is an absolute ride that needs to be experienced.

---

# A Unique (Not Cheesy) Setup
{: .text-center}

For the more inquisitive of readers who wonder, “What the heck could possibly be the story of Pizza Tower?”

![Pizza Face]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/pizzatower-pizzaface.jpg){: .align-center}
*Pizza Face, one of the main antagonists of the game.*
{: .text-center .small}

The game thrusts the player into the shoes of Peppino Spaghetti, the anxiety ridden, perpetually stressed owner of his business Peppino’s Pizzeria. Peppino is visited by a giant, sentient floating pizza (with a face) who explains that his business will soon be destroyed by a death ray. Said death ray being located at the top of the ominous tower across from Peppino’s Pizzeria. Peppino immediately springs into action, running straight for the tower to save his pizzeria. The rest of the game takes place inside of the tower. Yes, that is it. This is the whole setup for the game, and I think it is perfect.

---

# Starting from Floor 1
{: .text-center}

Pizza Tower plays like a 2D platformer comparable to the Wario Land series. This shouldn’t be much of a surprise considering the Wario Land series is the direct inspiration which Pizza Tower draws from.

![Peppino fighting Pepper Man]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/pizzatower-gameplay_1-1.jpg){: .align-center}
*Peppino fighting the first boss, Pepper Man.*
{: .text-center .small}

The game presents its levels through each floor of the pizza tower. There are 5 floors in the game and each floor has its own levels, boss battles, and themes. The player starts on floor 1 and to progress they must collect pizza toppings in the levels to acquire money. Having enough money allows the player to unlock the boss room, which upon defeating the boss will grant them the key to the next floor.

---

# Grade-A? S? or P? Gameplay
{: .text-center}

Pizza Tower stands out from the rest of the genre not only because of its 90s cartoon-like art style, smooth character animation, and fantastic soundtrack. Its solid and engaging gameplay is what keeps me coming back for more.

![One of the levels in Pizza Tower]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/pizzatower-gameplay_1-2.jpg){: .align-center}
*There are many enemies and obstacles to face in Pizza Tower.*
{: .text-center .small}

Each level has the player navigate through a linear environment, fighting enemies and overcoming obstacles as well as collecting items along the way. The end objective being the player destroys a pillar, then frantically races a timer back to the beginning of the level to escape.

However, what makes Pizza Tower so engaging is how each level complements Peppino’s diverse move set. Movement is an important element in this game as Peppino’s movements are tight and complex while being easy to understand. Peppino can perform a high speed ‘Mach run’ to break through metal boxes and barrel through enemies. He is also able to jump, super-jump, slide, redirect the direction of his mach run and much more. All of these movements combined turn Peppino into a devastating unstoppable force of nature.

![Run to the beginning of the level in time!]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/pizzatower-gameplay_1-3.jpg){: .align-center}
*Players must make a mad dash to the end before time runs out.*
{: .text-center .small}

What really sells the game is the replay value of each level. There are sub-objectives for each level that the player can perform to unlock achievements. There is also a ranking system for how well a player performs in each level. The ranks are D, C, B, A, S, and finally P.

P, standing for platinum, is the highest rank a player can achieve for a level and is definitely a fun challenge for those looking to 100% the game. All of these elements melt together into a palatable and fresh take on the 2D platforming genre.

![S-Rank Victory Screen]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/s-rank-pizzatower.jpg){: .align-center}
*The highest score is S-Rank, besides platinum.*
{: .text-center .small}

Pizza Tower is hands down the best indie game of 2023 and I highly recommend giving it a look!
